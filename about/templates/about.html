Kenmare Rugby Football Club HISTORY

Kenmare RFC has been playing an instrumental role in the development of rugby for boys and girls in the South Kerry region since its foundation in 2009. Training is available for ages U6, 8, 10, 12 and 14 years, and all boys and girls are welcome, especially newcomers and beginners. The emphasis is on fitness, fun and participation, and there is no contact playing at U6-U8 level and restricted contact at U10 – U12. The full pitch game is introduced at U14 -15 levels. Player safety is vital and all coaches are at least level 2 Munster branch approved.

  • Everyone that trains will get a chance to play in all games at underage rugby.
